About Me

I am a Master’s student in Statistics at the University of Missouri–Columbia. I graduated Magna Cum Laude with B.S. degrees in both Statistics and Mathematics. My research interests span high-dimensional methods, Bayesian statistics, and machine learning applications in economics and business. I am currently preparing PhD applications for Fall 2026.
